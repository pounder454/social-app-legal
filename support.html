Getting Started

Creating an Account

  1. Download Social from the App Store
  2. Tap "Sign Up" and enter your email and password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile with a name and photo
  5. Allow location permissions to find nearby venues

Checking In to a Venue

  1. Open the app and browse the map
  2. Tap on a venue marker or select from the list
  3. Tap "Check In" (you must be within 50 meters)
  4. You'll automatically check out after 4 hours or when you move 50m away

Adding Friends

  1. Tap the friends icon from the main menu
  2. Search for friends by username or email
  3. Send a friend request
  4. Once accepted, you'll see their check-ins and activity

Common Questions

Why can't I check in to a venue?

You must be within 50 meters of the venue to check in. Make sure:

  • Location services are enabled for Social
  • You've allowed "While Using the App" or "Always" location access
  • You're physically at or very near the venue
  • Your GPS signal is strong (try going outside if indoors)

How do I get checked out?

You'll automatically check out when:

  • You move more than 50 meters away from the venue
  • 4 hours have passed since your check-in
  • You manually tap "Check Out"

Can I use the app if I'm under 21?

Yes! While many venues are bars, the app also shows restaurants and other establishments. The app is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content, but can be used for finding restaurants and cafes.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't open

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your account data is saved)

Location not updating

  • Check Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services
  • Ensure Social has "While Using" or "Always" access
  • Try toggling location off and back on
  • Make sure you're not in Airplane Mode

Can't send messages

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular data)
  • Make sure you're friends with the person you're messaging
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
