Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Social ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, full name, username, date of birth, and profile photo (optional).
  • User Content: Messages, forum posts, and other content you create within the App.
  • Profile Information: Status messages and other profile details you choose to share.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: We collect your precise location when you check in to venues. Location tracking only occurs when you actively use the check-in feature.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version (collected when you submit bug reports).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including crash reports and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features and functionality
  • Enable you to connect with friends and other users at venues
  • Display your location status to friends when checked in
  • Allow you to post and view content in venue forums
  • Facilitate private messaging between users
  • Process and respond to bug reports
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, username, profile photo, status) is visible to other users
  • When checked in, your location is visible to your friends
  • Your forum posts are visible to all users checked in to the same venue
  • Your messages are visible only to the recipients

3.2 Service Providers

We use Google Firebase to provide backend services including:

  • Authentication and user account management
  • Database storage (Firestore)
  • File storage for profile photos
  • Crash reporting and analytics

Firebase may process your data in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights or the safety of users.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Forum posts are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Private messages in venue chats are deleted when all participants check out
  • Bug reports are retained for diagnostic purposes

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by contacting us at team.getsocial@outlook.com. Upon deletion, your account data will be permanently removed.

5.2 Location Services

You can disable location access in your device settings. However, this will prevent you from using check-in features.

5.3 Blocking Users

You can block other users to prevent them from interacting with you in the App.

6. Age Requirements

You must be at least 13 years old to use Social. Some features (such as messaging non-friends) require users to be 18 or older.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules to protect your data.

8. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Backend services, authentication, database, storage
  • Google Maps: Venue location display

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.
